Welcome to Room Allotment Manager

Our Hostel Management System provides students an easy way to manage their life at hostels by just Registering and filling the Application form and the Rooms are provided according to their last SEM aggregate .


  • Only SE , TE and BE students have to Apply Online .
  • Selected Students then can further Proceed with the Official paper work.
  • If students Fail to Apply Online then they have to contact the Warden / Rector of Respective Hostels.
  • Students with any kind of Disciplinary case Have to Contact the Warden / Rector Before Applying Online.
